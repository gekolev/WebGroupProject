We are one big happy family.

La Regina was founded in 2013 by the friends Mario Steffano and Luigi Roberto. After spending years in London they found that outside of Little Italy it tended to be difficult to find genuine high quality Italian dishes. That was when they decided to open up their own restaurant that valued quality and tradition in their way of cooking. Their vision was to show their guests genuine Italian food made from the freshest ingredients using family recipes that has been past down for generations.
